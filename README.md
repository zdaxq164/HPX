The standout feature of HPX is the HyperCard, a cryptocurrency-backed prepaid card that bridges the gap between digital assets and real-world spending. Users can deposit crypto assets like USDT, BTC, or ETH into their HPX wallet, convert them to USD or EUR, and load them onto a HyperCard. HPX's off-chain wallet provides rich functionality including asset value-added features, instant exchange services, collateral loans, market tracking tools, and multi-signature co-management for enhanced security. The platform also provides access to DeFi ecosystems through DApp integration across multiple chains including ETH, TRON, EOS, BSC, HECO, Polygon, Avalanche, Fantom, Solana, and more. The security strategy is built on an SBC risk control system, making it a preferred choice for users prioritizing both convenience and security in their digital asset management.

Recently, HPX announced a strategic partnership with Walle to integrate AI-powered intelligence into crypto wallets, transforming traditional storage tools into smart on-chain agents that can analyze market conditions and execute predefined strategies autonomously.
